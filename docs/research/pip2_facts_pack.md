# PIP-II facts pack (2026-07-05 deep dive)
23 cryomodules / 5 types; HWR 1x8 (162.5 MHz, 2.1->10 MeV), SSR1 2x8 (325),
SSR2 7x5 (35->185 MeV), 13x 650 MHz elliptical CMs to 800 MeV; ~125 LLRF-
controlled cavities; 215 m. Front end: 30 keV source (below-neutron-threshold
2.1 MeV RFQ rationale), 4-vane 4.45 m RFQ, emit 0.15 mm-mrad / 0.7 keV-ns.
Booster: charge-stripping foil, 285 turns per 0.55 ms pulse, transverse
painting. LLRF 0.065%/0.065 deg over ~125 cavities. CW-capable ex-cryoplant.
Sources: PIP-II RDR 2015, CDR 2017, pip2.fnal.gov, arXiv:2510.21036 (LLRF),
fermilab-conf-22-517 (cryoplant), arXiv:2208.11753 (PIP2IT).
